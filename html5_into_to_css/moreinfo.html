Forex Market

The foreign exchange market is an over-the-counter marketplace that determines the exchange rate for global currencies. It is, by far, the largest financial market in the world and is made up of a global network of financial centers that transact 24 hours a day, closing only on the weekends. It's amazing to think the stock market normally trades around 100-300 billion dollars a day, where as the Forex market being traded globally has an average of three Trilion plus dollars traded daily.
