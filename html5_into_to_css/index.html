What is Trading and Investing?

Trading involves buying and selling assets such as stocks or crypto for short-term gains. Traders primarily focus on share prices as they make their decisions. Investors, on the other hand, focus on long-term gains when they buy and sell investments.

Stock and Crypto Exchanges

A stock exchange trades in company stocks or shares, while a cryptocurrency exchange trades in cryptocurrencies (digital currencies), such as Bitcoin, Ethereum and many more.
