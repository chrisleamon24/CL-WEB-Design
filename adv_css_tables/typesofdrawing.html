Drawing Styles

There are multiple drawing styles that may peak interest. Some of the most popular and common ones are doodling, cartoons, photorealism, and line drawing. The key is finding a passion or style that you enjoy and working on those skills. It takes time an patience but the benenfits are worth it in the long run. Best thing to do is just grab a peice of paper and pencil and try it! One of my favorite styles is skecting with a pencil using lines.

Line drawing, just as the name suggests, entails using lines to create a picture. The style is simple and elegant. Beginners prefer line drawing as it is easy to create and takes a shorter time compared to other styles like the use of dots or shading.

Drawing Tools & Methods

You can use many different tools for drawing, and you can draw on literally anything. As you navigate the page and learn more about drawing think about what style you want to use. There isn't a correct or wrong style it's really up to you! Treat it as a hobby and passion and find your own excitment. Below are some of the most popular drawing tools with regard to your style:

  • Pencil drawing (graphite, watercolor pencils, or colored pencils)
  • Pen drawing
  • Crayon drawing
  • Ink drawing
  • Charcoal drawing
  • Chalk drawing
  • Digital drawing
